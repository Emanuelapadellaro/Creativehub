The
Moonshift

Artisanal Mezcal · Oaxaca · 2024

Rebranding and visual identity for The Moonshift, an artisanal mezcal distilled in the heart of Oaxaca. The visual language draws on the celestial, the ancient, and the handcrafted — translating the magic and tradition of agave spirits into a system that feels rare and unmistakable.

About the project

The new label design is rooted in printmaking tradition — combining hand-drawn botanical illustration with striking typographic architecture. Each element communicates the essential character of the mezcal: 100% Maguey Espadín, 46% Alc., 750ml. "A Spark of Magic and Tradition in Every Glass." The identity translates seamlessly from bottle to brand communications, creating a world as layered and artisanal as the spirit itself.

Bacab
Magazine

Rincones de Ensueño · Vol. 01 · Septiembre 2024

About the project

Editorial design for the inaugural issue of Bacab Magazine — 'Rincones de Ensueño: El Romance del México Bohemio Contemporáneo.' A 68-page exploration of art, architecture, design, and community in Mexico, conceived as a space where tradition and modernity meet in perfect harmony. From the cover to interior spreads, every detail was considered to give the publication a distinctive identity that reflects the cultural richness of its content.
